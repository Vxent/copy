Varieties of RiceHub.

Long grain (Basmati, Jasmine): Fluffy separate grains, good for pilaf, stir-fries. Medium grain (Arborio): Slightly sticky, ideal for risotto and sushi rice. Short grain (Sushi): Very sticky, perfect for sushi and rice pudding. Brown: Whole grain with bran intact, nutty flavor, more fiber. Black: Deep black color, chewy texture, high in antioxidants and fiber. Wild: Not true rice, aquatic grass seed, nutty flavor, longer cook time.
